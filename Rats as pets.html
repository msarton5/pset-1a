Rats as Pets

Rats also make excellent pets! The average pet rat lives for about 2-3 years.

You of course need to feed your pet rat. Rats are omnivores, so they need a diet of both fruits/vegetables and meat. Some fruits/vegetables you can feed your rats are:

  1. Bananas
  2. Apples
  3. Citrus fruits
  4. Celery
  5. Carrots

Rat pellets and cubes provide a mix of many things a rat needs, such as protein and fiber.
