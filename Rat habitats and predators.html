Habitats and Predators

Habitats

A common misconception is that all rats live in the city! While New York City is infested with rats, rats can be found in other places too. You can find rats anywhere; but they are typically found in dark, wet areas. Rats are commonly found in both rural areas as well as urban areas.

Predators

Although they may seem ferocious, rats have many predators. These predators include...

  • Hawks
  • Falcons
  • Owls
  • Humans
  • Snakes
