Rats

Habitats and Predators

A common misconception is that all rats live in the city! While New York City is infested with rats, rats can be found inother places too. You can find rats anywhere; in the forest, in the marshland, in the countryside, in your basement, and even by the ocean!

Although they may seem ferocious, rats have many predators. These predators include large birds of prey, like hawks or falcons, and humans.
