Rats

Habitats and Predators

A common misconception is that all rats live in the city! While New York City is infested with rats, rats can be found inother places too. You can find rats anywhere; but they are typically found in dark, wet areas.

Although they may seem ferocious, rats have many predators. These predators include...

  • Hawks
  • Falcons
  • Owls
  • Humans
